Common Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Vendor won't provide SOC2 report

Solution: Request security questionnaire or alternative attestation. If they refuse, consider replacing them or accepting the risk with executive approval.

Challenge: Too many vendors to assess

Solution: Start with critical/high-risk vendors only. Tackle medium-risk vendors over time. Use automation to track.

Challenge: Vendor's SOC2 report shows exceptions

Solution: Document how exceptions impact your risk. Implement compensating controls if needed. Request remediation timeline.

Challenge: Can't get responses from legacy vendors

Solution: Escalate to account manager. If still no response, document attempts and consider replacement.
